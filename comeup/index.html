Dealmaking Platform

COMEUP is more than just a stage — it's a real investment platform. Global VCs and CVCs join to connect directly with startups, opening doors to follow-on investments and strategic partnerships. From initial meetings to tangible outcomes, experience an investment-driven stage at COMEUP.

Thematic Deep Dive

COMEUP 2026 has structured each day around three key themes: Technology, Global, and Public Engagement. From the latest industry trends to the firsthand experiences of founders, the program delivers focused and in-depth insights through a selective and concentrated approach.

Global Nexus

With global rounds spanning the U.S., China, Japan, and Europe, COMEUP is expanding into a true global nexus where startups from around the world come to Korea, and Korean startups venture out to the world. A journey that goes beyond Korea to connect with the globe—this begins at COMEUP.
